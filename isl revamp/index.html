INTERNATIONAL SCHOOL LAHORE

Our Vision

School Lahore aims to serve the community by fostering the perpetual spirit of inquiry and resilience leading to discovery and achievement. We envision education to be a steady revolution that celebrates the talents of individuals and harnesses their collective energy for social change.

Our Mission

International School Lahore strives to provide the best educational opportunities, resources, and possibilities for our students. We endeavor for excellence in mentor-ship and teaching through our faculty, coaches, counselors and mentors; our human assets. Our students are respectful, honest and empathetic. They value the happiness and well-being of others as they become change-makers and thought-leaders in their communities to better the lives of people around them.
